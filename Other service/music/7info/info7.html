Oxygen World Fusion Music Band


Oxygen World Fusion Music Band is a wedding music company based out of Chennai. Based out of Chennai in India, formed in 2003, this super talented band conceptualises live music and blends various music genres including Carnatic, Hindustani, Classical, Indian Folk, Rock and Blues.

Their team has produced over 45 tracks including 4 music albums. The band gained popularity after winning a TV show judged by Oscar award winner A R Rahman, who then produced and released their next single.

Oxygen World Fusion Music Band's team is known for their maniacal performances and their ability to drive the crowd crazy with their beats and aura. Their team works and travels globally so distance is never a problem.

No matter where you plan to tie the knot with your significant other in the country, they can come to your location and perform at your pre-wedding, wedding and post-wedding ceremonies.
