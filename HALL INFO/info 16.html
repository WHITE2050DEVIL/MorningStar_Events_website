Brindan Hall


Brindan Hall, Goa is a fabulous venue for hosting pre-wedding functions, weddings, grand reception, birthday parties, bachelorette parties, cocktail party, and many more functions. The chilled and lovely weather of Goa adds to the beauty of the celebration.

The venue has various options from mid to large gathering banquet halls for varied functions. Brindan Hall, Margao, Goa is a great place to party with your friends, family and all the near and dear ones.

The venue is located close to Old Station Road in Margao which is a popular place in Goa and makes the arrival of guests pretty smooth.
